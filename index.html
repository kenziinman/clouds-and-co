Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, this is a real service. No, we don't know how either.

Can I keep the cloud?

No. Clouds are living, breathing entities (sort of). All rentals must be returned to the atmosphere within the agreed timeframe. Extended parking fees apply at $25/hour.

What if my cloud rains on me?

That's a feature, not a bug. However, unscheduled precipitation is covered under our "Oops, It Poured" guarantee. We'll send a complimentary umbrella and a $10 credit.

Do you ship internationally?

Clouds don't respect borders, and neither do we. We deliver to 43 countries. Note: customs may inspect your cloud. This is normal and only mildly humiliating for the cloud.

Is this real?

We prefer the term "aspirationally real." Our legal team says we should clarify that no, you cannot actually rent a cloud. But isn't the dream beautiful?

Can I get a cloud shaped like my face?

Our artisan cloud sculptors can accommodate most requests. Face-shaped clouds start at $499. Results may vary based on wind conditions and how photogenic your bone structure is.
